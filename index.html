Tony Stark aka "Iron Man"

Iron Man who under all the armor is Tony Stark, a brilliant engineer. He created his suit to protect him and to give him extra ordinary strength and powers. His ability to fly and shoot beams of energy from his hands and chest help him defeat his foes. The suit protects him from damage and gives him superhuman strength. Tony has adapted to challenges he has faced to over come even the most challenging tasks due to his true genius nature.

Tony's upcomming role as a member of the Avengers will surely be exciting and if the movies stay true to the comics, Tony and the iron suit will be come one. He will have to use his most powerful weapon to survive, his mind. Soon we shall hopefully see Tony and the iron suit truly become Iron Man.
